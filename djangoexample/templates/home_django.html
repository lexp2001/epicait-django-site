Epica Model

Epica model creates Software Products from problem solving, to Tech Capital, steering the development cycle and relieving StartUp founders from hand holding so you can use that time to build your business.

EPICA’s HR model takes cues from “Direct trade”. our philosophy is cemented in Conscious Capitalism, we aim to bring opportunity to talented people all over Latin America in a way that is meaningful to them. Responding to the notion that today's world is witnessing one of the biggest transformations that may have either devastating effects on LATAM societies if opportunities remain out of reach or, could mean that talent drain no longer needs to happen, it is 2019 and opportunity can be anywhere!

Being a 100% remote company also means the talent pool extends to every corner of Latin America and is not circumvented by any particular tech cluster, allowing us to source and hire expert talent in an elastic way according to project needs and integrate them into Epica’s technology-agnostic process that guarantees the delivery of “Technology Capital” you can actually use to lean your company growth against.
