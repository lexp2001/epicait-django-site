What would you like to build?

So, you’ve been contemplating the idea of nearshore but seems almost impossible to find the right mix of technology, quality code, volume, and price for your early stage StartUp development needs? We know the standard nearshore model is not inherently the right fit for the agility, level of innovation and focus on KPI’s a Startup requires & this is what makes us radically different from other nearshore options: WE SPEAK STARTUP.


Our growth strategy is not based on headcount, we know Startups need their IT investment to stretch and retract according to revenue / investment flow. We aim to become a Nearshore Seed Accelerator & that means the brands we help build today, will help us build the reputation and create the Investment Fund we will need in the future, therefore for us, it is every bit as much about your successful exit as it is for you

Let's create together!

Do you consider yourself an innovator? Would love to work independently, are capable of collaborating with professionals anywhere in the world and look at mobility as a key factor for work-life balance? Epica’s business model takes cues from “direct trade” and runs on a system called ROWE (Results only work environment) which allows you to work anytime, anywhere as long as consistently deliver results sprint by sprint.


After having to move to pursue opportunity, we realized that there’s a lot of people like us, wishing they did not have to move. We decided that being 2019 and all, we needed to bring the right kind of opportunity to talented folks right where they want to live, whether that be the sandy beaches of Florianopolis, Brazil, or wine country in Ensenada, Mexico, allowing them to be happier and more prone to innovation.
